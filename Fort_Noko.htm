Fort Noko

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia.

Established around 1000 AD in west coast of Finland, aka "Noko Castle" (Nokon Linna). During Finland's semi-independence 1000-1200 Fort Noko was minor defence post against the Hurri ie the Swedes. Mentioned in Timo Noko's family tree referring to the documents dated 1222 AD by Bishop Thomas

May also refer to "Nokola" (the House of Noko) established by Erik of Pomerania in 1407 AD in southwestern part of Finland.
